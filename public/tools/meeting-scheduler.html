The Problem with Manual Scheduling

You've probably done this before:

  1. Calculate what 3 PM your time is in your colleague's timezone
  2. Create a Google Calendar event
  3. Add a note: "2 PM PST / 5 PM EST / 10 PM GMT / 3:30 AM IST (next day)"
  4. Hope everyone reads the note and converts correctly

Result? Confusion, missed meetings, and wasted time.

How Meeting Scheduler Solves This

Our tool handles timezone conversion automatically:

  1. Pick your timezone and set the meeting time
  2. Add attendee timezones to see what time it'll be for everyone
  3. Click "Add to Google Calendar"
  4. Done! Google Calendar shows the correct local time for each person

✨ Magic of Timezone-Aware Calendar Events

When you create an event with proper timezone data:

  • Someone in New York sees "2:00 PM EST"
  • Someone in London sees "7:00 PM GMT"
  • Someone in India sees "12:30 AM IST (next day)"

No manual notes needed. Google Calendar does the conversion automatically!
