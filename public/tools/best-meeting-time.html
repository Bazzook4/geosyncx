The Global Meeting Challenge

Scheduling meetings across timezones is painful. You spend 10 minutes checking if 2 PM your time works for:

  • Your colleague in London (is it too late?)
  • Your client in India (is it their morning or night?)
  • Your developer in San Francisco (are they even awake?)

By the time you find a slot that works, you've wasted valuable time and still aren't sure if it's ideal.

How Best Meeting Time Finder Works

1. Add All Participants

Enter the timezones of everyone who needs to attend. You can search by city, country, or phone code.

2. Set Working Hours

Define what "working hours" means for each timezone (default: 9 AM - 6 PM local time). This ensures you don't suggest 3 AM meetings!

3. Get Instant Recommendations

The tool analyzes all timezones and shows you time slots where everyone is within their working hours.

💡 Real Example

Participants: New York, London, Mumbai, Tokyo

Best Meeting Times:

  • Option 1: 8:00 AM New York = 1:00 PM London = 6:30 PM Mumbai = 10:00 PM Tokyo ❌ (Too late for Tokyo)
  • Option 2: 6:00 AM New York = 11:00 AM London = 4:30 PM Mumbai = 8:00 PM Tokyo ✅ (Acceptable for all)
  • Option 3: 9:00 PM New York = 2:00 AM London ❌ (Too late for NY, too early for London)

The tool highlights Option 2 as the best choice automatically!
