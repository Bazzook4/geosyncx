Quick Reference

  • IST Timezone: GMT+5:30 (Asia/Kolkata)
  • GMT Timezone: GMT+0 (UTC)
  • DST: Neither timezone observes daylight saving time

GMT vs UTC

GMT and UTC are effectively the same for most practical purposes. UTC is the modern standard used by servers and international systems, while GMT is the traditional timezone based on the Prime Meridian in Greenwich, London.
