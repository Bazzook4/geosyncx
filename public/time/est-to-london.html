About Eastern Time to London Time Conversion

London time (GMT) is 5 hours ahead of Eastern Standard Time (EST). When it's noon in New York, it's 5:00 PM in London. During British Summer Time (BST), London is 5 hours ahead, but during EDT the difference is 4 hours.

Quick Facts

Eastern Time is in the America/New_York timezone (GMT-5/-4)

London is in the Europe/London timezone (GMT+0/+1)
