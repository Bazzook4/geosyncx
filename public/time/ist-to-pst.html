IST to PST Time Converter

Convert Indian Standard Time to US Pacific Time (San Francisco/Los Angeles) instantly.

Key Insight: This is one of the most challenging timezone pairs for real-time collaboration. When it's noon in India, it's 10:30 PM the previous day in California.

Quick Reference

  • Time Difference: California is 13.5 hours behind India (IST)
  • IST Timezone: GMT+5:30 (Asia/Kolkata)
  • PST Timezone: GMT-8 (America/Los_Angeles)
  • During PDT: Difference reduces to 12.5 hours (March-November)

Best Times for India-California Calls

The optimal overlap is 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM IST, which corresponds to 6:30 AM - 9:30 AM PST. Late evening in India catches early morning in California.

The Famous "8:30 AM PST Standup": If your California team has standup at 8:30 AM PST, that's 10:00 PM IST - a common pain point for India-based engineers working with Silicon Valley companies.
