Best Meeting Time Finder: Find Perfect Overlap Across Multiple Timezones

Published: November 4, 2025 · 10 min read · Category: Meeting Tools

Stop manually checking if 3 PM works for everyone. Automatically discover meeting times where all participants are within their working hours.

The Overlap Problem

With team members in San Francisco, London, and Singapore, finding a time when everyone is awake (let alone at work) is nearly impossible. The timezone spread can be 16+ hours!

How Best Meeting Time Finder Works

  1. Add all participant timezones to GeoSyncX
  2. The tool analyzes working hours (9 AM - 6 PM) for each timezone
  3. It finds overlapping windows where everyone is available
  4. Results are color-coded: green (all available), yellow (some outside hours), red (bad time)

Features

  • Customizable working hours per timezone
  • Visual timeline showing overlap
  • Suggestions ranked by quality
  • One-click to schedule selected time

When Perfect Overlap Doesn't Exist

Sometimes there's no perfect time. The tool helps you find the "least bad" option and shows exactly who would be inconvenienced.
