EST to PST Time Converter

Convert US Eastern Time (New York) to Pacific Time (Los Angeles) instantly.

Quick Reference

  • Time Difference: Pacific Time is always 3 hours behind Eastern Time
  • EST Timezone: GMT-5 (America/New_York)
  • PST Timezone: GMT-8 (America/Los_Angeles)
  • DST: Both zones change together, maintaining the 3-hour gap

Best Times for Coast-to-Coast Calls

The overlap is excellent with just 3 hours difference. 12:00 PM - 5:00 PM EST (9:00 AM - 2:00 PM PST) works perfectly for both coasts during business hours.

Common Use Cases

  • Coast-to-coast US business coordination
  • Nationwide conference calls
  • TV broadcast scheduling
  • Sports event timing
  • National retail operations

Pro Tips

  • Always specify the timezone when scheduling US-wide meetings
  • East Coast meetings at 9 AM mean 6 AM for West Coast - not ideal
  • Midday EST works best for both coasts
