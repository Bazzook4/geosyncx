Quick Reference

  • Time Difference: IST is 13.5 hours ahead of PST
  • PST Timezone: GMT-8 (America/Los_Angeles)
  • IST Timezone: GMT+5:30 (Asia/Kolkata)
  • During PDT: Difference is 12.5 hours

Best Times for Silicon Valley-India Calls

PST early morning (7-9 AM) catches IST evening (8:30-10:30 PM). This is the standard "standup" window for Bay Area-Bangalore teams.

The Famous "8:30 AM PST Standup"

Many Silicon Valley companies with India engineering teams hold daily standups at 8:30 AM PST, which is 10 PM IST. Bangalore is often called "the city that never sleeps" due to US timezone sync requirements.
