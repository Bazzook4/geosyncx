Best Times for Calls Between India and London

The optimal overlap for business calls is 1:30 PM - 6:30 PM IST, which corresponds to 8:00 AM - 1:00 PM London time. This ensures both parties are within standard working hours.

Daylight Saving Time Note

London observes British Summer Time (BST) from late March to late October. During BST, clocks move forward 1 hour, reducing the time difference to 4.5 hours. India does not observe DST, so this change only affects the UK side.
